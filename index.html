Educational Tour — Cebu & Bohol

Day 1

Worldtech Information Solutions Inc.

Observations: Worldtech showed itself as a strong and reputable IT training and consultancy company with branches in Cebu, Manila, and other major areas. They place a big emphasis on cybersecurity and offer certification programs taught by highly experienced professionals.

Learnings: I learned the importance of hands-on training in developing real-world IT skills. Their strong focus on cybersecurity highlighted how essential it is for organizations to stay protected against digital threats.

Day 2

Rivan IT and Codechum

First stop: Rivan IT

Observations: Rivan IT Cebu’s training center is well-organized and highly professional. Their networking and certification courses are handled by skilled instructors who build strong technical foundations.

Learnings: I learned how structured training environments help students gain real-world competencies and confidence in the IT field.

Second stop: Codechum

Observations: Codechum is a user-friendly coding platform with automated checking and multi-language support, making it great for students and teachers.

Learnings: I learned how instant feedback improves coding skills and how Codechum helps reduce cheating by monitoring submissions effectively.

Day 3

MATA Technologies, Inc.

Observations: MATA Technologies showcased impressive innovation in virtual reality and digital tours. Their team demonstrated immersive VR experiences tailored for tourism and real estate.

Learnings: I learned how VR technology can elevate industries by offering interactive and accessible digital experiences.

Day 4

TaRSIER 117

Observations: TaRSIER 117’s emergency response system is highly organized and well-prepared. Coordination with government agencies showed their strong commitment to public safety.

Learnings: I learned how proper planning, teamwork, and emergency readiness greatly improve community safety.
